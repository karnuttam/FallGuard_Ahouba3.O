CRITICAL RISK

Visually impaired individuals are 3x more likely to experience falls than sighted peers.

Current Challenge

Problem Statement

Limited Environmental Awareness

Reduced visual cues make it difficult to detect obstacles, uneven terrain, or steps, significantly increasing the risk of accidental falls.

Delayed Assistance

Post-fall disorientation or injury often prevents manual calls for help. "Long lie" periods (>1 hour) lead to severe complications.

THE NEED

A smartphone-based system that detects falls automatically, sends SOS alerts with location, and requires minimal user interaction.
