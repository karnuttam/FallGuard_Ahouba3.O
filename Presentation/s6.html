DETECTION LOGIC

How Fall Detection Works

1

Free Fall Stage

The accelerometer detects a sudden drop in acceleration towards zero gravity as the phone falls.

Threshold Condition

Acceleration < 0.5g

2

Impact Stage

Immediately follows free fall. A sharp spike in acceleration occurs when the body hits the ground.

Threshold Condition

Acceleration > 2.5g

3

No Movement Stage

The system monitors for a period of inactivity post-impact to confirm the user is unable to get up.

Threshold Condition

Stillness ≥ 10 sec

Verification Logic

IF (Free Fall + Impact + No Movement) = TRUE

TRIGGER SOS ALERT
