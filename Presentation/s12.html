PRODUCT ROADMAP

Future Improvements

Projected Timeline: Q3 2024 - Q2 2025

01
Short Term Ecosystem Integration

Smartwatch Integration

Extending detection logic to WearOS and Apple Watch. Wrist-based sensors provide higher accuracy for hand-movement related falls and easier access to SOS controls.

Wearable API

Cloud Dashboard

A centralized web portal for caregivers and doctors to view historical fall data, activity logs, and configure sensitivity settings remotely.

Firebase
02
Mid Term Advanced Intelligence

ML Fall Prediction

Moving beyond threshold detection to predictive analysis. Using LSTM neural networks to analyze gait anomalies and predict fall risk before it happens.

TensorFlow

AI Voice Assistant

Deep integration with Google Assistant/Siri for conversational checks. "Hey Google, I'm okay" to cancel alarms or "Help me" to trigger them.

NLP / Voice SDK
03
Long Term Holistic Safety

Health Monitoring

Correlation of fall data with real-time heart rate and SpO2 levels to distinguish between simple trips and medical emergencies (e.g., cardiac arrest).

Bio-Sensors

Indoor Navigation

Using Wi-Fi RTT and Bluetooth beacons to guide visually impaired users through complex indoor environments, reducing collision risks.

Indoor Mapping
